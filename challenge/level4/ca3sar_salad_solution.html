Solution for level 3 - Caesar cipher frequency analysis


You were presented a picture of Asterix and Obelix the lone heroes against Caesar, or are they ? Caesar used a cipher called, the caesar-cipher (how creative) he used to decrypt the messages he sent. As seen in the picture below, the alphabet gets moved by a certain amount of characters, to make the message unreadable. Example:
key: 3
A -> D
B -> E
C -> F

So the text will end up scrambled, however out of modern day view, this method of encryption is not safe anymore. There are multiple possible ways to solve this level, first one being brute forcing the key until you get a text that makes sense since there are only 26 possible keys, this would'nt take that long. However there is another more, thought-through method than guessing with brute force.

The so called Frequency Analysis

where you basically look at the distribution of the letters in the text and then compare them to the englisch language.

As seen below this is the frequency of letters in the English language
